Game Rules 🎲

The player who gets to 100 points first wins 🏆. You can roll the dice 🎲 (when it is your turn), as much as you want but as soon as you roll 1, all your current points will be lost 😜, and the turn goes to the other player. When you press the hold button 🔘 , your current score is added to your total score and the next player gets to play.

May The Dice Be With You 🎲 !
